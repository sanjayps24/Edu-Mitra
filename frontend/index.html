How It Works

From Data to Action in 4 Steps

1

Input Academic Data

Teachers enter student attendance %, assignment scores, midterm and final exam marks via easy-to-use forms.

2

ML Model Analyzes & Predicts

XGBoost processes features, applies trained model, and classifies the student as Low, Medium, or High risk with confidence score.

3

Personalized Recommendations

Students see targeted study tips and improvement suggestions based on their weakest academic areas identified by the AI.

4

Automated Alerts & Intervention

High-risk students trigger automatic email alerts to teachers. Admins view system-wide trends to drive institutional policy decisions.
